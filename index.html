What is HRCP?

HRCP provides HR certification prep and continuing education (CE) courses for professionals in PHR, SPHR, SHRM-CP, and other certification programs.

How do I complete CE courses online?

You can complete CE courses online through HRCP or SecuritiesCE. Each course includes lessons, quizzes, and a final exam to earn credits.

Are study guides available?

Yes! HRCP offers downloadable study guides, practice exams, and flashcards to help you prepare efficiently for your certification exams.
